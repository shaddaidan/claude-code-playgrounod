HOW TO PLAY

  1. A sequence of colored tiles will flash on the grid.
  2. Wait for the entire sequence to finish playing.
  3. Click the tiles in the exact same order.
  4. Each correct round adds one more tile to the sequence.
  5. Every 5 rounds = level up. Tiles flash faster. Your brain panics. Fun!

🧠 THE SCIENCE BIT

This game trains your working memory — the mental sticky note your prefrontal cortex uses to hold information while you act on it. Researchers link strong working memory to better focus, reasoning, and resistance to distraction. So technically you're doing homework right now.

(Studies also show that failing repeatedly at memory tasks builds character. Probably.)

🏆 SCORING

Base score: sequence length × 10

Combo multiplier: starts at 1×, goes up by 0.5 every 3 wins (max 4×)

Wrong answer: multiplier resets. Oops.
